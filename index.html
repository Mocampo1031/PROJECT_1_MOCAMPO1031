but for some reason the floor mats in the car look really small

like two tiny carpets for invisible hamsters
I notice it while waiting at a red light
The seat is huge
the dashboard is huge
the steering wheel feels like a planet
but the floormats…
still small
I look down again
just to be sure
they sit there quietly
like they know something
I don’t
maybe the car got bigger
maybe the floor mats shrank
maybe I’m just thinking too much
at a red light
the car is normal
the floor mats are suspicious
